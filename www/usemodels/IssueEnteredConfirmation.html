Screen Description: IssueChangedConfirmation

Name: IssueEnteredConfirmation
Purpose: A panel that can be shown on several screens (namely: IssueView, ProjectIssues) that confirms that a issue was entered or changed and shows who was notified of the change. This is a feature similar to one in bugzilla.
Contents:
  1. Label indicating successful change
  2. issue number
  3. list of people notified
  4. list of dependent issues?
  5. link back to that issue
